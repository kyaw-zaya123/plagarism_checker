User Guide

How to Use the Application

  1. Registration:

    To use the application, first create an account by clicking on the "Register" link on the login page. Fill in the required fields and submit the form.

  2. Login:

    After registration, log in using your credentials. Enter your email and password, then click the "Login" button.

  3. Uploading Files:

    Once logged in, you can upload files to compare. Navigate to the dashboard and click the "Upload" button to select files for comparison.

  4. Viewing Results:

    After the comparison process, results will be displayed on the screen. You can view the similarity percentage and comparison details.

  5. History:

    Your comparison history is saved and can be accessed from the "History" page. You can delete individual records from this page.

  6. Logout:

    When you are done, remember to log out by clicking the "Logout" link.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: What file formats can I upload?

    A: You can upload text files (.txt), PDF files (.pdf), and Word documents (.docx).

  • Q: How is the similarity percentage calculated?

    A: The similarity percentage is calculated based on text analysis algorithms comparing the uploaded files.

  • Q: Can I delete my comparison history?

    A: Yes, you can delete individual records from your history page.
